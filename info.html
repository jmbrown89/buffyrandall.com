Buffy is a writer, illustrator and scientist based in Boston, Massachusetts. She draws on her experience as a scientist to inspire children’s books about the everyday science around us.


Her debut picture book - Poppy’s Big Idea - is about communicating difficult ideas and working together to achieve brilliant things.
